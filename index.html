Peppermint
Breath Freshener / Antimicrobial

Beyond its well-known breath-freshening effect, peppermint contains menthol and menthone compounds with demonstrated antimicrobial activity against oral pathogens. Clinical evidence indicates it can reduce volatile sulphur compounds, the primary chemical cause of chronic bad breath.

Spearmint
